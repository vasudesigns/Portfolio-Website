My Story

I’m a UI/UX designer from Autin, TX!

I bring together logic, empathy, and visual storytelling. With a background in pharmaceutical industry, I learned to value detail and precision—skills I now apply in digital product design. I enjoy working on apps that make life easier and more meaningful, especially for families and everyday users. Whether I’m mapping out user journeys or perfecting UI spacing in Figma, I stay focused on solving real problems with thoughtful design.

My career shift wasn’t accidental—it was driven by a love for learning, a desire to create, and a strong sense of purpose. I bring empathy and a user-first mindset to every project, with a keen interest in mobile-first design, accessibility, and simplicity. My design process is shaped by curiosity, research, and iteration. Outside of work, I love reading about child development and exploring visual storytelling.
