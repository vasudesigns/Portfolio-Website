SaveIt

Project 2: SaveIt – Money-Saving App

This app helps users set saving goals and track spending in a motivational, friendly way. I focused on designing simplified flows, emotional rewards, and a visual system that builds trust and habit.

View Portfolio
  • Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Duration: 3 weeks
  • Tools: Figma, InVision, FigJam

Process

  • Research: I learned users struggled with unclear financial language and a lack of motivation.
  • Design: I created a simple onboarding flow, visual progress tracking with badges, and a color palette that evokes trust and motivation.
  • Iteration: Feedback led to redesigning the reward system for more emotional reinforcement.

Conclusion

Savlet makes financial progress feel achievable and engaging—supporting long-term savings habits and business goals.
