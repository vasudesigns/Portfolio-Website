Gigglegram - Baby Tracking App

Overview

Gigglegram is a baby-tracking app designed to help busy parents log routines, track milestones, and discover daily age-based activities—all in one place.

  • Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • Tools: Figma, InVision, FigJam

Process

  • Research: I conducted interviews with parents to identify pain points with current baby apps. I discovered that users were juggling multiple apps and feeling overwhelmed.
  • Design: I mapped out a user flow, created low-to-high fidelity wireframes, and designed a calming, clean UI with age-appropriate suggestions.
  • Testing: Usability tests led to iterations, such as simplifying routine logging and adding visual milestone progress.

Conclusion

Gigglegram improved parental engagement and satisfaction by providing everything in one place—helping parents feel more informed and less stressed.
